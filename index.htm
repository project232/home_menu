DSEU’s Rajokri campus truly complies with the essence of DSEU’s initiative to offer quality vocational higher education and training in all areas of Delhi, ensuring that students of all regions have a DSEU campus within a few kilometres of their residence. The campus has a great infrastructure to support world-class training of students, strengthening them to become excellent in their vocation. Well-linked to the Aerocity Metro Station, with last-mile connectivity through electric rickshaws and other modes, students can easily arrive at the centre. Only a few kilometres from Gurgaon, a major cyber city of India, the students have a tremendous variety of opportunities to choose from, each of which can be realised from any of DSEU’s courses. The campus is also only a few minutes away from Aerocity and Mahipalpur, buzz spots to many 5-star hotels, such as Radisson Blu, Roseate House, JW Marriot and many more along with branded retail outlets and luxurious cuisines. The region is also filled with large wedding venues, adding to the available opportunities in Hospitality. The campus with its well-equipped infrastructure, modern tools and technology aimed at introducing a variety of new courses, benefitting the students in securing high-yielding opportunities in the future. The vibrant and welcoming environment makes the Rajokari campus an ideal space for the learning, growth and development of students and professionals. The campus has a total 6 full-time faculty members with 4 contractual faculty members and 2 lab technicians/ technical assistants.
